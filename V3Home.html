Tawa Community Centre

The Tawa Community Centre is a large, welcoming facility centrally located in the heart of Tawa. The centre is used on a regular basis by many community groups for activities such as dancing and fitness classes, art classes, seminars, toastmasters and holiday programmes. You can also drop in to the centre during opening hours to enjoy a cup of coffee and a chat.

Linden Social Centre

The Linden Social Centre is a welcoming facility in the heart of Linden, run by the Wellington City Council. It has a hall and the Doris Mills Lounge for hire. The centre is used on a regular basis by many community groups. The coordinator operates out of the Tawa Community Centre. Activities include: Te Mahuri Te Reo Maori (Whanau) group, Tawa Mahjong, Tawa Lions, Tawa College Community Ed Pilates, Barnardo's PreSchool Music, Nurtured at Home Music, Bharatanatyam Dance Classes, U3A classes, Boxfit, Church groups and private bookings.

Tawa Library

There's more than you think at the local Mervyn Kemp (Tawa) Library. Whether you want to tackle a new sport, find out about your favourite hobby, learn a new language or just find a great read, this library will help you out. Send your mind on an exciting journey, as you explore the wonderful world of books and more. The Tawa Library offers a huge range of services including browsing and study facilities; Magazines, local history collections with references materials and newspapers for use; and even access to the Internet and other electronic resources.
